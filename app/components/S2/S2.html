What is
creative distraction?

  • It is a technique to move your attention away from negative thoughts to something else that would serve you better.
    1. You may picture that delicious bar of chocolate in the cupboard, even sensing the taste of it until it becomes simply irresistible. Then your body goes into action, almost with a will of its own, robot-like! Too late! The diet is broken
    2. Some distraction was needed early in this sequence to prevent the ‘unwanted behaviour’ occurring. Hence the name creative distraction technique.
  • Activity of any kind is usually a fairly good solution for distracting yourself in any circumstances, distraction methods can be:
    1. Physical – using bodily movements and sensations
    2. Mental – refocusing the mind on alternative thoughts and images
  • Creative distraction can be practised in neutral, non -threatening, no risk situations. Doing it this way you can develop the skills of distraction and then use them ‘live’ in situations where you need them, such as sticking to a wellness resolution
